You play a game where you and another player see the same four images but in different orders.
You also get a description from the other player describing one of the images.
Your task is to identify the image based on the description.
You both win if you pick the correct image based on the other player's description.

Click on the button with the number (1, 2, 3, 4) of the image that the given description matches.

You will play 6 games in a row.
