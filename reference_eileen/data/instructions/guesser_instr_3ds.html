You play a picture guessing game where you and another player are given the same four images but in a different order.

Alongside the images, you will be given an expression made by the other player that describes one of the images.

Your task is to identify the image the expression is referring to.

Click on the button with the number (1, 2, 3, 4) of the image that the given description matches.

You will play 9 games in a row.
