You play a picture guessing game where you and another player are given the same four images but in a different order.

Your task is to describe the image labeled "Target" so that the image stands out against the others.

The other player will see your description alongside the images and will have to identify the image you are referring to.

Type in the description for the image labeled "Target" and hit send.

You will play 10 games in a row.
